Welcome
to AFSAxCOD

AFSAxCOD is a website designed to connect the world of online gaming with real-life social impact, specifically targeting support for AIDS Foundation South Africa (AFSA). The platform integrates charitable activities into the gameplay of Call of Duty, leveraging the gaming community’s engagement to generate funds and awareness for AFSA’s mission.


How to join in the fun

Click the signup icon at the top of the website to stay updated with the Call Of Duty:AFSAxCOD challenges.

    How to donate to the organization

    Click the heart and hand icon at the end of every page.
